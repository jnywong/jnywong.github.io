This is Jenny Wong,
postdoctoral researcher
at ISTerre.

Hello my name is Jenny and I am a postdoc at ISTerre, Grenoble, France. I am interested in fluid dynamics and use numerical simulations and theory to answer questions related to the dynamics of the Earth's core. At ISTerre I am working with Renaud Deguen on core-mantle differentiation of planetary bodies, a project funded by the ERC.

I completed my PhD at the EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Fluid Dynamics at the University of Leeds, UK. Together with Chris Davies (School of Earth and Envirornment) and Chris Jones (School of Mathematics), we developed a slurry model to explain how the F-layer may be stably stratified.

Following this, I did a postdoc at IPGP, Paris, France, working with Julien Aubert to examine the effect of the F-layer on convective processes that drive the geodynamo, which was funded by La Fondation Simone et Cino Del Duca.
