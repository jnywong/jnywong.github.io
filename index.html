Hello my name is Jenny and I am a postdoc at IPGP, Paris, France. I am interested in fluid dynamics and use numerical simulations and theory to answer questions related to the dynamics of the the Earth's core. At IPGP I am working with Julien Aubert to examine the effect of the stably stratified "F-layer" on the convective processes that drive the geodynamo.

I completed my PhD at the Centre for Doctoral Training in Fluid Dynamics at the University of Leeds, UK. Together with Chris Davies (School of Earth and Envirornment) and Chris Jones (School of Mathematics), we developed a slurry model to explain how the F-layer may be stratified.
